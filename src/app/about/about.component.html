David D'Agostino

Senior Director of Engineering

I am a Senior Director of Software Engineering living in Washington D.C. who has grown obsessed with cryptocurrency ever since reading about Bitcoin back in 2010. In looking for a website to track price changes, research coins, and manage my portfolio, I decided it would be more fun to build something on my own. Using Angular 4, Material Kit Pro, PHP, MySQL and a slew of apis, I developed this single page application to satisfy both my itch to code and my curiosity for cryptocurrencies. I hope you enjoy!
